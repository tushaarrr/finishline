# FinishLine — Demo Voiceover Script (3 Minutes)

> **Pacing note:** ~150 words/minute = ~450 words total. Read naturally, not rushed.

---

**[0:00 – 0:20] HOOK — Landing Page**

This is FinishLine. An AI-native personal CFO that connects to your real bank accounts—and tells you exactly what to do with your money before you even think to ask.

Let me show you how it works, live.

---

**[0:20 – 0:50] CONSUMER LOGIN + DASHBOARD**

We're logging in as a regular user. This is the consumer dashboard. Everything here is real—pulled directly from Plaid. Net worth, connected accounts, pending opportunities. No mock data, no hardcoded numbers.

The AI has already scanned this user's accounts and found opportunities. Here's one: "You may have unused TFSA contribution room." It calculated the exact impact—three hundred fifty dollars a year in tax-free returns—and wrote a plain-English brief explaining why. One tap to approve, one tap to skip.

---

**[0:50 – 1:15] SALARY AUTOPILOT + CHATBOT**

Now here's what makes FinishLine different. When your salary lands, the AI detects it automatically and suggests an optimal split. Eight hundred to RRSP—saves you two sixty-four in taxes. Six hundred to TFSA. Seven eighty toward credit card debt. One tap to execute all four moves.

And if you have questions, the chatbot is a full financial agent. Ask it "Should I buy NVDA?"—it pulls real-time stock data. Say "Move five hundred to my TFSA"—it stages the action, confirms, and executes. Say "I want a human"—it escalates directly to your advisor.

---

**[1:15 – 1:45] SWITCH TO ADVISOR SIDE**

Now let's flip to the advisor panel. Same database, same clients, completely different view.

This is Client Triage. Every client sorted by risk—urgent, high, all clear. The advisor sees the full picture without opening a single spreadsheet.

Here's Health Scores. Each client scored zero to one hundred across five dimensions: emergency fund, debt, investing activity, spending discipline, salary consistency. This client is at sixty out of a hundred—at risk. The advisor can drill into the breakdown and see exactly why.

---

**[1:45 – 2:15] DORMANT + ENGAGEMENT**

Dormant Reactivation. The AI identifies clients who haven't acted in sixty-plus days but still have pending opportunities and real balances. It pre-writes a personalized message from their actual data. The advisor reviews, edits if needed, and sends with one click. That message shows up as a purple notification on the client's dashboard within seconds.

Engagement Analytics. Every client's opportunity approval rate, broken down by tier—Champion, Engaged, Disengaged, Never Acted. The advisor instantly sees who needs attention and who's self-managing.

---

**[2:15 – 2:40] SENDING A LIVE ALERT**

Let me send a real alert right now. I'm clicking Send Alert on this client. The modal opens with a pre-filled message about their TFSA room. I hit Send. Toast notification confirms it. If I switch to this client's consumer dashboard—there it is. Purple banner, real-time, from the advisor. They dismiss it, and it stays dismissed across sessions.

---

**[2:40 – 3:00] CLOSE**

Everything you just saw runs on real Plaid data, real Supabase queries, and real AI. Claude writes the briefs. OpenAI embeddings power the RAG knowledge base. Zero mock data.

One advisor. Full awareness of every client. That's FinishLine.

---

> **Total: ~440 words | ~3 minutes at natural pace**
